FOOD AS MEDICINE

Eating well enhances ones innate healing capacity.

A nutrient dense whole food diet has the power to prevent and treat a myriad of illnesses from autoimmune disease, asthma, and eczema to cancer, diabetes, heart disease, and stroke. Using food as medicine it is quite possible to reduce ones dependence on medications.


Foods to Eat Often

Berries, such as blueberries, strawberries, and raspberries

Colorful vegetables of all kinds, organic whenever possible

Green leafy vegetables, including collards, spinach and kale

Beans and legumes - lower cholesterol levels, and act to control blood sugar levels

Meats, naturally raised, include a variety beef, pork, lamb, bison, venison, etc.

Seafood - salmon, trout, sole, sardines, shellfish and shrimp

Whole fat dairy products. Fermented yogurts and kefirs are high in probiotics

Whole grains, in the whole form, quinoa, brown rice, steel-cut oats.

Herbs, garlic, rosemary, turmeric, Italian herbs, parsley, chili, curry spices.

Cruciferous vegetables, cabbage, broccoli, bok choy, cauliflower, and kale

Nuts: almonds, walnut, pecans, macadamia, hazel nuts etc.

Tea: green, black, oolong

Chocolate and cocoa, at least 75% dark

Extra virgin olive oil
