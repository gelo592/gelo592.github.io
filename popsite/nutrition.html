Food as Medicine

Healthful eating can turn your body into a healing machine. With proper nutrition it is possible to return to health by removing harmful agents and replacing healing nutrients and healthy bacteria.

Decreasing the Need for Medication

Medications are designed and tested for short term use but frequently end up as part of a daily regimen for years, sometimes with negative effects that are not evident until years later. Using integrative medicine, it is our goal to reduce or eliminate your need for some medications.

16 Foods to Eat Often

For better health and longevity, incorporate the following into your regular diet:

  1. Green leafy vegetables, including collards, spinach and kale.
  2. Lean - not mean, protein; chicken, turkey breast. Nonfat dairy products are excellent lean protein sources.
  3. Seafood - salmon, trout, sole, sardines, shellfish and shrimp.
  4. Beans and legumes - lower cholesterol levels, suppress appetite and help to control blood sugar levels.
  5. Berries, such as blueberries, strawberries, and raspberries.
  6. Soy products - soy milk, veggie hot dogs and burgers, soy sausage or soy pepperoni, tofu.
  7. Whole grains such as wild rice, quinoa, brown rice.
  8. Herbs and spices: rosemary, Italian hers, parsley, chili, curry spices.
  9. Garlic is a wonder spice - best if minimally processed. Smash and mince it, do not overcook.
  10. Nuts: almonds, walnuts, pecans all lower cholesterol and one handful a day will help with weight control. (Unsalted and no added oils.)
  11. Cruciferous vegetables: cabbage, broccoli, bok choy, cauliflower, kale remove toxins and are loaded with anti-aging nutrients.
  12. Nonfat yogurt is loaded with calcium and helps to build a healthy intestinal tract.
  13. Red wine raises healthy HDL levels, decrease disease, diabetes and clotting. In moderation, limit to two servings a day.
  14. Tea and infusions such as ginger tea and green tea.
  15. Flaxseed: ground is packed with fiber and helps with peri-menopausal symptoms.
  16. Chocolate and cocoa are packed wit anti-aging nutrients and decrease stress and assist with healthy artery formation.
