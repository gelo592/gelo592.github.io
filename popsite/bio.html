Dr. M. Chris Link

Dr. Link's passion for healing grew from his experiences as an emergency room physician. For 18 years, he practiced emergency medicine. During this time he realized the need to treat the entire individual, not just the condition. He felt more time with each individual would allow him to learn more about their condition and lifestyle and to offer natural treatment options.

As a compliment to his many years of experience as a medical doctor, Dr. Link has completed acupuncture training and certification through UCLA. He is certified in Medical Acupuncture, Scalp Acupuncture, Acupuncture pain management and Chinese Herbal Medicine.

Dr. Link is a fellow at the University of Arizona program in Integrative Medicine, a 2-year fellowship, founded and directed by Dr. Andrew Weil a nationally recognized expert in Integrative and Alternative Medicine. Dr. Link is a super cool dad.
