Cholesterol & Heart Disease

In addition to the classic risk factors for heart disease, we utilize advanced

cholesterol panels and inflammation markers, and when necessary advanced imaging, to stratify each individual's heart disease risk and to optimize their treatment.

Classic Risk Factors

  • Family History
  • Elevated Cholesterol
  • Smoking
  • Diabetes
  • Hypertension

Advanced Cholesterol & Inflammation Markers

  • Lipid Particle size and number
  • Inflammation markers - CRP, MPO, Fibrinogen
  • Homocysteine
  • Hemoglobin A-1-C
  • Testosterone

Advanced Non-Invasive Imaging

  • Carotid Intimal Media Thickness
  • Coronary Calcium Scan

Low Level Laser Therapy (LLLT)

LLLT is a form of laser medicine used in medical therapy that uses low-level (low-power) lasers or light-emitting diodes to alter cellular function, lessen pain, and speed healing.

Advanced Functional Medicine Testing


Tests we offer:

  • Food sensitivities
  • Nutritional assessment of vitamins, minerals, aminoacids, and dietary fats
  • Stool analysis of digestion, GI bacteria, yeast and parasites
  • Small Intestinal Bacterial Overgrowth - SIBO breath test
  • Hormone evaluation for men and women
  • Adrenal Stress Index
  • Heavy metal analysis
  • Advanced cholesterol risk assessment
  • Autoimmune evaluation
  • Complete thyroid profile

Cupping
