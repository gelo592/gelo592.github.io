Low-Level laser Therapy

The application of a light to promote tissue repair, reduce inflammation, and provide pain relief.

How it Works

LLLT is where you take specific wavelengths of light and project them thru the skin to get healing benefits and pain relief. It is painless! Think of LLLT is like photosynthesis in plants - where plants need sunshine to grow and stay healthy. LLLT causes a photochemical response that leads to a cascade cellular energetic changes, enhanced healing and decreased pain.

There are hundreds of scientific studies on LLLT. Several are ongoing at prestigious institutions like Harvard. The device is FDA approved in the United States. It is also approved in Europe and Canada.

There are 4 common clinical targets for LLLT:

  1. The site of injury to promote healing, remodeling and reduce inflammation
  2. Lymph nodes to reduce edema and inflammation in the region
  3. Nerve tissue to induce analgesia or pain relief
  4. Muscle trigger points to reduce tenderness and relax contracted muscles

Examples of conditions treated:

  1. Muscular back and neck pain
  2. Sciatica
  3. Osteoarthritis and sore joints, e.g., wrist, elbow, shoulder, hip, knee, ankle
  4. Shingles pain
  5. Poor wound healing
  6. Neuropathy of hands and feet

The number of treatments required is variable. The faster an injury is treated, the faster it resolves. Typically two or three times weekly will be optimal, often achieving considerable improvement in 3-4 weeks.
